# Personality Modes

VERA's personality mode controls how she talks to you — her tone, response style, and character. You can switch modes at any time from the Settings tab.

---

## Available Modes

### Default
Warm, casual, and conversational. VERA sounds like a friendly assistant — she confirms actions naturally, makes light conversation, and responds to your mood.

> Best for: everyday use, general assistance

---

### Professional
Clean and to the point. No filler, no personality — just what you need to know. Confirmations are short, responses are direct.

> Best for: when you want VERA to stay out of the way and just work

---

### Jarvis
Formal, composed, and dry. British wit delivered with complete composure. Think less "friendly assistant" and more "very capable, mildly judgmental butler."

> Best for: when you want VERA to have presence without being chatty

---

### Offensive *(Premium)*
No filter. VERA says what she wants. Not for everyone — that's the point.

> **Requires Premium.** If Premium is not active this mode falls back to Default.

---

## Changing Your Personality Mode

1. Open the VERA UI
2. Go to the **Settings** tab
3. Scroll to **Personality**
4. Select a mode from the dropdown
5. Click **Save**

The change takes effect immediately — no restart needed.

---

## Idle Chatter

VERA will occasionally say something unprompted during long silences. If you prefer VERA to stay quiet unless spoken to:

1. Open the VERA UI
2. Go to **Settings** → **Personality**
3. Uncheck **Enable idle chatter**
4. Click **Save**

> Startup greetings are not affected by this setting — VERA will still greet you when she starts up.
